Hi. I'm Niki.

A senior UI/UX designer who specializes in B2B applications where business requirements and user needs intersect. My work focuses on transforming complex workflows into intuitive interfaces that drive measurable business outcomes.

My Background

I've spent 15+ years designing digital products, with particular expertise in custom business applications. What distinguishes my approach: I don't just make things pretty—I architect solutions that balance user needs, technical constraints, and business objectives.

My background in both design and development gives me unique perspective on what's technically feasible and strategically valuable. I understand the full product lifecycle from concept to deployment.
